We are Kingsfield College
KINGSFIELD COLLEGE was established on the 22 nd of September, on in the year, 2006. The Main Campus and the Annex of the College are located in serene environments in Ikorodu, Lagos State, Nigeria. The college is presently one of the fastest growing international schools in Nigeria with students drawn from within and outside the shores of country.

The rapid growth of the school is due, largely, to her competent members of staff, hybrid curriculum and the availability of ultra-modern facilities such as:
. Science laboratories,
.Technical Drawing studio ,
.E-library ,
.Lecture Theatres,

ABOUT section

Our school curricular system is a fusion of three distinctive curricula, namely Nigerian SSCE curriculum, British (Cambridge) IGCSE curriculum and the American (College Board) SAT curriculum. Our integrated curricular system exposes our students to a wide range of academic contents. Through this, they have unhindered access to university admission opportunities in Nigeria, United State of America, United Kingdom and many other countries in Europe and Asia.We possess desirable assets and facilities that will ensure premium quality education like;
Music Lab; Our multi media music laboratory is equipped with updated musical instruments. Our zealous, creative and versatile music directors are readily available to arouse your child interest and develop your child talents with top-of-the-line music technology.
science laboratories;The college provides an engaging and dynamic learning experience for science subjects. With separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Agriculture Science, and Biology, students have the opportunity to actively explore and conduct experiments. These well-equipped labs enhance their understanding, develop critical thinking, and foster a passion for scientific inquiry. Experienced teachers guide students through practical exercises, ensuring a strong foundation in science education.
Library;The College boasts a fully equipped library that caters to the needs of all its students. Our library houses a wide array of thoughtfully selected materials tailored to suit students of different ages and academic levels. In addition to textbooks, we regularly supply a variety of newspapers and magazines to keep both staff and students informed about current affairs. Our extensive collection includes multiple textbooks that cover all subject areas in the Secondary School Curriculum.And we also boast of many other facilities in our academicinventory.
